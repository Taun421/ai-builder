Automation Roadmap

What I'm Building

Sorted by impact: hours reclaimed × alignment with winning new clients. Highest first.

Priority 01
Big Build ~4–5 hrs/wk
Every prospect gets a personalized benefits tool demo before the first meeting.
What's happening today
Each client benefits site takes hours to build manually in Claude — done after engagement begins, not as a door-opener.
Automated version
Prospect intake form → Claude auto-generates branded microsite → deployed to Tiiny Host → sent as first follow-up. The tool is the pitch.
Not Started Directly tied to #1 goal
Priority 02
Quick Win ~3 hrs/wk
LinkedIn leads are worked without you thinking about it.
What's happening today
LinkedIn sends 45+ lead suggestions per week. They land in inbox and mostly go unworked — no outreach follows automatically.
Automated version
Weekly Claude agent scores leads by fit, drafts personalized first-touch messages referencing a specific tool you've built. You approve, not compose.
Not Started Pipeline builder
Priority 03
Medium Lift ~3–4 hrs/wk
Quote spreadsheeting for renewals happens in minutes, not hours.
What's happening today
Carrier renewal quotes (like UHC) are manually laid out side-by-side every renewal cycle — a recurring task across the entire book of business.
Automated version
Forward a carrier quote PDF → Claude extracts plan details → auto-populates comparison spreadsheet in your format. Review for accuracy, then send.
Not Started Prototype started Apr 24
Priority 04
Quick Win ~2 hrs/wk
Google Alerts become a weekly prospect intelligence brief — not inbox noise.
What's happening today
3–4 alert emails arrive daily (~80/month) across benefits broker and group benefits topics. Read and deleted — no system extracts actionable signals.
Automated version
Friday Claude agent reads week's alerts → extracts named companies switching brokers, industry talking points, and one news hook for cold outreach.
Not Started Intelligence → pipeline
Priority 05
Medium Lift ~2 hrs/wk
Client follow-up after a demo or quote happens without you remembering.
What's happening today
After sending a prospect a tool demo or quote, follow-up depends on memory. No CRM visible. SkyWest — what's the next step, and is there a date on it?
Automated version
Prospect email sent with attachment → Claude logs contact, drafts a follow-up for 3 days later, surfaces it for your approval. Requires Outlook integration.
Not Started Needs work email MCP
Priority 06
Quick Win ~1 hr/wk
Gmail inbox is pre-sorted every morning — only work-relevant items surface.
What's happening today
Amazon shipments, grocery pickups, medical notices, and utilities arrive alongside LinkedIn leads and industry alerts — all in the same flat inbox.
Automated version
Gmail filters route transactional email to labeled folders. Morning Claude agent surfaces 3 items needing action. Clean inbox, zero scanning. Bundle with P04 brief.
Not Started 20-min setup in Gmail
